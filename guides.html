Proper Gym Basics

Key Principles:

  • Posture: Maintain correct posture during lifts. Never use weight that will compromise form.
  • Breathing: Make sure to inhale and brace your core before performing a rep. Exhale throughout hardest part of the lift.
  • Range of Motion: Always use full range of motion when possible and keep movements controlled.
  • Weight Selection: Start light and focus on form. If you can perform 10-12 reps easily with good form, then consider increasing weight.
  • Rest: Take 1-2 minutes between sets. Heavier intensity should take 2-4 minutes.
